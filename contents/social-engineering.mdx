## What is Social Engineering?

**Social Engineering** is a non-technical form of attack that relies on human interaction. The attacker tricks people into breaking normal security procedures or revealing confidential information.

## Common Techniques

- **Impersonation**
- **Urgent requests**
- **Guilt or fear pressure**

## Best Practices

- Conduct regular employee awareness training
- Use multi-factor authentication
- Verify identities through multiple channels
- Report suspicious interactions
