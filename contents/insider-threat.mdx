## What is an Insider Threat?

An **Insider Threat** is a security threat posed by individuals within the organization, such as employees or contractors. They may intentionally or accidentally cause harm by leaking, stealing, or sabotaging sensitive assets.

## Types

- **Malicious insiders** – Deliberate harm
- **Negligent insiders** – Human error
- **Compromised insiders** – Hacked accounts

## Best Practices

- Monitor privileged access
- Educate staff on secure behavior
- Deploy Data Loss Prevention (DLP) tools
- Conduct regular audits and access reviews
