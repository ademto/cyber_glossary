## What is Compliance?

**Compliance** ensures that an organization meets legal and industry standards for handling and protecting data. Non-compliance can result in fines, lawsuits, or reputational damage.

## Key Areas

- **Data privacy regulations**
- **Industry frameworks**
- **Internal policy enforcement**

## Best Practices

- Assign a compliance officer
- Use compliance management tools
- Conduct regular audits
- Stay updated with evolving laws
